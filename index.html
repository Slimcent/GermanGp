Its important to know the German equivalent of your Grade Point before proceeding with your German school Application.

Note

Your Grade Point : This is the grade point you graduated with, the grade point in your transcript.

School GPmax : This is the Highest or Maximun grade in your school grading system.

School GPmin : This is the Lowest or Minimum grade in your school grading system, its usually 1.

1 is the Highest or Maximum grade in the German grading system, 1 is First class.
